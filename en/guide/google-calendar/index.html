What is the purpose?

This site take inspiration from "unitncal" project by fgaz. The site basically does a url replacement to generate the link to the calendar in ical format. The timetable calendar can then be added to your app via the functionality "calendar subscription".

This site does a trivial job and there are others ways to get the same link.
